Hello! We are team "Ecliptic" researching 12 topics for YRT!

This platform serves as an interactive repository for the scientific research conducted by team Ecliptic for the Young Researchers Tournament (YRT). Here, we present our comprehensive analysis, theoretical models, and experimental data for 12 distinct problems spanning physics, chemistry, and interdisciplinary sciences. We aim to make our scientific findings accessible and engaging. We invite you to browse through our active workspace and review the results of our work.
