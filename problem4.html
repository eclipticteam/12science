Salty boiling water

Problem Formulation:

The boiling point of water changes upon the addition of dissolved salts; however, under real-world conditions, this dependence can deviate significantly from idealized models. Investigate how the concentration and chemical nature of a salt affect the boiling point of the solution in an open system. How do evaporation processes, time-dependent concentration changes, salt solubility, and interionic interactions influence the stability and dynamics of boiling?
